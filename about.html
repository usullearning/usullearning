Who We Are

About Usul Learning

An independent initiative dedicated to making authentic Islamic knowledge accessible through structured, scholarly educational resources.

Our Mission

Making Authentic Islamic Knowledge Accessible

Usul Learning was founded with a singular purpose: to present Islamic knowledge with the clarity and structure that modern learners need, without compromising the authenticity that centuries of Islamic scholarship demand.

The name Usul refers to "foundations" or "roots" in Arabic — capturing the essence of what this project aims to do: return to the authentic foundations of Islamic practice as transmitted through the scholarly tradition.

All material is derived from the Qur'an and authentic Sunnah, presented according to the understanding of Ahl al-Sunnah wal-Jamāʿah within the Shafi'i madhab. The goal is faithful transmission of established scholarly knowledge — not personal interpretation.

Muhammad Faheem
Author & Educational Director
About the Author

Muhammad Faheem

Muhammad Faheem is the founder and author behind Usul Learning. Committed to presenting authentic Islamic teachings in a clear and structured manner, he developed this initiative to help Muslims — especially those learning independently — to understand and correct their worship.

The project reflects a personal conviction: that access to sound Islamic knowledge should not be a privilege, but a right available to every Muslim who seeks it — regardless of background or location.

For feedback, corrections, or suggestions, readers are welcome to reach out at contact@usullearning.com.
