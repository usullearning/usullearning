About Usul Learning
"Knowledge is a light that guides the seeking heart — and the first step is always to ask."

Usul Learning was established to present Islamic knowledge with clarity while preserving the authenticity of the scholarly tradition.

All material is based on the Qur'an and authentic Sunnah, as understood by Ahl al-Sunnah wa'l-Jamā'ah within the Shafi'i madhhab. The focus is on the clear and faithful transmission of established knowledge.

Our Approach

Grounded in Tradition

Every piece of content follows a structured scholarly framework built upon the Islamic tradition preserved over centuries.

Qur'an & Sunnah

All teachings derived directly from the Qur'an and authentic Sunnah — the primary sources as understood by the scholarly tradition.

Shafi'i Madhab

Legal rulings follow the established methodology of Imam al-Shafi'i and the generations of scholars who preserved and developed this school.

Scholarly Integrity

Faithful transmission of established knowledge — not personal interpretation or innovation. What is taught reflects what has been preserved and transmitted by the scholars.
