Founder of Usul Learning · Islamic Education Platform
-----------------------------------------------------

I am the founder of Usul Learning, an educational initiative focused on providing structured Islamic knowledge based on the Qur’an and authentic Sunnah within the Shafi'i tradition.

My work centers on developing clear, accessible, and well-organized learning materials in a bilingual Arabic-English format. The goal is to build a long-term system of knowledge through carefully structured volumes.

Alongside this, I design and develop web platforms to deliver educational content efficiently, combining technical implementation with structured learning methodology.

* 🌍  I'm based in Kerala, India
* ✉️  You can contact me at [contact@usullearning.com](mailto:contact@usullearning.com)
* 🧠  I'm currently learning Usul Learning platform and Volume 1 publication
* 👥  I'm looking to collaborate on Educational platforms, structured learning systems, and web solutions
* 💬  Ask me about Building a long-term educational project with multiple structured volumes. Focused on clarity, authenticity, and accessibility in learning.
