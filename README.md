## 📖 About

> *"And say: My Lord, increase me in knowledge."* — Qur'an 20:114

**Usul Learning** is an Islamic education platform built on the conviction that authentic knowledge deserves a structured home.

The mission: transform scattered, fragmented Islamic learning into **organized, accessible, and enduring educational content** — rooted in the Qur'an, grounded in the authentic Sunnah, and articulated through the Shafi'i tradition.

This is not a content feed. It is a **knowledge architecture**.

## 🧠 Core Principles

**I. Structure over scatter** — knowledge must have form and sequence to be retained.

**II. Authenticity over assumption** — every claim is traced to its source.

**III. Clarity over complexity** — the tradition made accessible without compromise.
