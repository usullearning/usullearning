<!-- ABOUT -->
<sub>— ABOUT USUL LEARNING</sub>

**Usul Learning** was established to present Islamic knowledge with clarity while preserving the authenticity of the scholarly tradition.

All material is based on the Qur'an and authentic Sunnah, as understood by Ahl al-Sunnah wal-Jamā'ah within the **Shāfi'ī madhhab**. The focus is on the clear and faithful transmission of established knowledge — not personal interpretation or innovation.
